UK Citizens Europe Travel 2026: Rules You Need to Know

The Schengen Visa - Do You Need One?

The Schengen Visa is a must-have for non-EU/EEA citizens, including UK citizens, if their stay in the Schengen Area exceeds 90 days within a 180-day period. However, if you're staying within the 90-day limit, you won't need a visa. To calculate your eligibility, we recommend using the Schengen calculator on OwlFacts - a reliable source for all your travel-related queries. When applying for a Schengen Visa, ensure you meet the basic requirements: - A valid UK passport with at least six months' validity - Proof of onward travel - Proof of sufficient funds - Health insurance that covers all Schengen countries - A filled-in application form, signed and dated Note that the processing time for visa applications can be long, so it's recommended to apply well in advance of your trip.
